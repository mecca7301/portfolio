Ugali Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • Pinch of salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Gradually add maize flour while stirring continuously.
  3. Keep stirring until thick (no lumps!).
  4. Shape into a round mound and serve hot with sukuma wiki or meat.

Serving Suggestion

Best served with sukuma wiki and grilled meat!

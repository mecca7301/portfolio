Nyama Choma (Grilled Meat)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg goat meat (ribs or thigh)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 lemon (juiced)

Instructions

  1. Marinate meat with oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and lemon juice for 1 hour.
  2. Grill over charcoal for 30-40 minutes, turning occasionally.
  3. Serve hot with kachumbari and ugali.

Pro Tip

For extra flavor, use Royco marinade spice!
